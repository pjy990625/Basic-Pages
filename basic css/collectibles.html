1. What are collectibles?

Items that is worth far more than its appears due to its RARITY & DESIRABILITY
The price for a particular collectible usually depends on how many of the same items are available as well as its overall condition.
Common categories of collectibles include antiques, toys, coins, comic books, and stamps.
People who amass collectibles take a lot of time to collect them, and usually, store them in locations where they will not be ruined.
